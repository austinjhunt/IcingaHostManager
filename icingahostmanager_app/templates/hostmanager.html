Welcome to the Icinga2 Host Manager

Here, you can easily add hosts for Icinga2 to monitor, either one at a time or in bulk via a CSV upload. New additions will trigger the deployment of a new Icinga configuration that includes monitoring for the hosts you've added. You will be asked to confirm the new addition after you submit one of the following forms. Please ensure all details are correct before confirming.
